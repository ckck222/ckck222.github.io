Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Kimchi fried rice is a popular Korean dish that combines spicy, tangy kimchi with savory fried rice. To make this dish, start by cooking rice and allowing it to cool. Then, saut√© diced onions and garlic in oil before adding chopped kimchi and cooking for a few more minutes. Next, add the cooked rice to the pan and stir-fry with the kimchi mixture. Finally, season with soy sauce and sesame oil to taste. Kimchi fried rice is often served with a fried egg on top and garnished with sliced green onions. This dish is a delicious and easy way to use up leftover rice and kimchi, and is perfect for a quick and flavorful meal.

Recipe

Ingredients:
  1. 2 cups cooked white rice
  2. 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  3. 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  4. 4 slices bacon, chopped
  5. 1/4 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed
  6. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  7. 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  8. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  9. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  10. 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  11. 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  12. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
Instructions:
  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the bacon and cook until crispy, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the chopped kimchi and stir-fry for another minute.
  5. Add the rice, peas and carrots, and kimchi juice to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the rice is heated through and the ingredients are well combined.
  6. Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and black pepper. Stir-fry for another minute.
  7. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve hot.
