Bulgogi Recipe

Bulgogi is a popular Korean dish that has become a beloved cuisine around the world. The dish features thinly sliced beef that is marinated in a sweet and savory sauce made with soy sauce, sugar, and other seasonings, and is often cooked on a grill or stir-fried. With its tender and juicy meat and delicious flavors, bulgogi is a must-try for anyone looking to experience Korean cuisine.

Recipe

Ingredients:
  1. 1 pound beef sirloin, sliced thinly
  2. 1/2 onion, sliced thinly
  3. 3 green onions, sliced thinly
  4. 1/4 cup soy sauce
  5. 2 tablespoons sugar
  6. 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  7. 2 tablespoons cooking wine
  8. 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  9. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  10. 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, mix together the soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, cooking wine, minced garlic, black pepper, and sesame seeds.
  2. Add the beef slices to the marinade and mix well.
  3. Cover and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. In a large skillet or wok, stir-fry the onions and green onions until they are slightly softened.
  5. Add the marinated beef to the skillet and stir-fry until the beef is cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with rice and your choice of vegetables.
