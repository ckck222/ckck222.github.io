Mango Bingsu Recipe

Mango bingsu is a popular Korean dessert that has become a popular treat around the world. Bingsu, also known as patbingsu, is a shaved ice dessert that is typically topped with sweetened condensed milk, fruit, and other toppings. Mango bingsu, as the name suggests, features sweet, ripe mango as the main ingredient. The dish is made by blending mango into a smooth puree, pouring it over a bed of shaved ice, and then drizzling sweetened condensed milk on top. The result is a refreshing and sweet dessert that is perfect for hot summer days. With its combination of sweet mango and icy shaved ice, mango bingsu has become a beloved treat for many, and is a must-try dessert for anyone looking to experience Korean cuisine.

Recipe

Ingredients:
  1. 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and cubed
  2. 2 cups shaved ice
  3. 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  4. 1/4 cup heavy cream
  5. 1 tablespoon sugar
  6. 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
Instructions:
  1. Put the mango cubes in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  3. Place the shaved ice in a bowl or plate.
  4. Pour the mango puree over the shaved ice.
  5. Drizzle the condensed milk mixture on top.
  6. Enjoy!
